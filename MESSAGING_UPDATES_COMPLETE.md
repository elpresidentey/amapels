## Brand Voice Guidelines

### **Words to Embrace**
✅ Exquisite, treasure, radiant, luminous, grace, artisan, crafted, curated, timeless, heirloom, celebrate, cherish, adorn

### **Words to Avoid**
❌ Basic, simple, cheap, trendy, accessory, item, stuff

### **Messaging Pillars**

1. **Artisan Craftsmanship**: Emphasize handcrafted, Lagos-based creation
2. **Emotional Resonance**: Jewelry marks moments, not just outfits
3. **Cultural Pride**: Celebrate Nigerian heritage and artistry
4. **Timeless Quality**: Pieces to treasure, pass down, cherish
5. **Personal Connection**: Each piece tells a story

---

## Tone Characteristics

- **Elegant yet Warm**: Sophisticated without being cold
- **Aspirational yet Accessible**: Premium positioning, welcoming approach
- **Confident yet Humble**: Expert without being pretentious
- **Emotional yet Clear**: Evocative but never vague
- **Cultural yet Contemporary**: Nigerian roots, modern expression
