# AMAPELS Messaging Audit & Recommendations

## Current State Analysis

### What's Working ✅
- **Elegant tone**: The current copy maintains sophistication
- **Nigerian pride**: Lagos craftsmanship is highlighted
- **Gift focus**: Gifting is mentioned throughout
- **Clean language**: Avoids overly flowery or clichéd jewelry language

### What Needs Improvement 🔧

#### 1. **Emotional Connection**
- Current: Functional descriptions ("pieces designed to catch the eye")
- Needs: Emotional storytelling ("pieces that capture life's most precious moments")

#### 2. **Luxury Positioning**
- Current: "Beautiful pieces" (generic)
- Needs: "Exquisite creations," "Timeless treasures," "Artisan craftsmanship"

#### 3. **Heritage & Craftsmanship**
- Current: Brief mentions of Lagos
- Needs: Deeper storytelling about Nigerian artistry and cultural richness

#### 4. **Desire Creation**
- Current: Descriptive ("light-catching pieces")
- Needs: Aspirational ("adorn yourself in radiance")

---

## Recommended Messaging Framework

### Brand Voice Pillars

1. **Elegant yet Warm**: Sophisticated without being distant
2. **Culturally Proud**: Celebrating Nigerian heritage
3. **Emotionally Resonant**: Jewelry marks moments, not just outfits
4. **Confident**: Premium positioning without pretension
5. **Personal**: Each piece tells a story

### Tone Guidelines

- **Use**: Timeless, treasure, heirloom, radiance, grace, artistry
- **Avoid**: Cheap, trendy, accessory, basic, simple
- **Embrace**: Crafted, curated, designed, inspired, celebrating
