### Simple Steps:

1. **Login to Admin:**
   - Go to: https://amapels.vercel.app/admin/login
   - Email: `admin@amapels.com`
   - Password: `Amapels2024!`

2. **Click "Add Product"**

3. **Fill in 5 fields:**
   - Name: "Beautiful Gold Bracelet"
   - Price: "₦180,000"
   - Category: Choose from dropdown
   - Description: Tell what makes it special
   - Images: Drag & drop photos

4. **Check "Feature on homepage"** (optional)

5. **Click "Create Product"**
